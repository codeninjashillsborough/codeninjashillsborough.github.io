Code Ninjas Parent Waiver

It is recommended this document is viewed on a computer or wider screen, as compared to a mobile phone.


The undersigned (s) being the lawful parent(s) and/or guardian(s) of the above-named Child(ren), hereby consent to the participation by the Child in learning activities, lessons, guidance, and care (the “Program”) conducted by Mercury2, LLC, a NJ Limited Liability Company doing business as (DBA) Code Ninjas, and its directors, officers, employees, agents, and authorized personnel (collectively, the “Provider”), and to the participation of the Child in all events related to said Program.


Services Provided

In exchange for the payment(s), assumptions of risk, releases of liability, and/or waivers of liability described below, Provider agrees to provide computer programming instruction (“curriculum”) and STEM activities for students ages 5-17. Students are to follow the rules of the dojo. If any student becomes violent, aggressive, disrespectful or uncooperative, grounds for dismissal shall be as determined in the sole discretion of Provider, and such dismissal shall not relieve Parent of any duties or payment obligations already incurred, nor entitle Parent to any refund. Furthermore, the Program requires the active participation of the Child in accordance with the Program’s curriculum. Please see the Code of Conduct for more info.


Payment for Services

Code Ninjas Create & Code Ninjas Junior Program: Payment for the Program(s) listed above shall be payable according to the terms selected. All memberships include up to 2 hours per week/ 8 hours of curriculum per month for the Regular Create Program and 1 hour per week/4 hours per month for the Junior Program. Additional coding hours beyond contract limit may be purchased at the rate of $30/hr. All membership sales are final, no refunds available; non-transferable. Students are expected to be picked up at the end of their hour; late pickups may incur additional charge for staff to remain onsite after closing.

Code Ninjas customer credit cards are charged at the beginning of each month. If a decision is made to withdraw from Code Ninjas during the payment period for any reason a refund may not be issued. If a Code Ninjas customer withdraws without giving proper notice as stated in the Parent Waiver, the credit card used may be charged in the amount of the current tuition, per child.


Month to Month Memberships:

All m2m membership subscriptions will auto-renew at the 'current prevailing monthly subscription price' on the next billing date - unless the parent contacts the center in writing to a) select a new Paid in Full membership -or- b) cancel membership by giving at least 30-days notice. Month to Month rates are subject to periodic increases.


Paid in Full (PIF) Memberships:

PIFs are not refundable and do not freeze; you can use the total hours by the last date of the term. Once the term ends, the hours may not be redeemed. You must contact the center to renew your PIF membership (at the prevailing rate at that time) or it will automatically default to the month-to-month rate (at that time). 


Sibing Discount:

Siblings of enrolled student receive a 5% discount on the Create Program. No discounts are to be combined.


Freezes:

One 30-day freeze allowable for m2m memberships per calendar year and must be given advance notice of 15 days before bill date. No hours may be used during a Freeze as the license is suspended. Freezes will automatically resume payment and reactivate on the end date in the system. PIFs do not Freeze; use hours by last date of PIF.


Missed Classes Policy:

Hours missed may be banked and made up as soon as possible (while actively enrolled). In event of membership cancellation, you must make up any hours by the last date of the membership or they are forfeighted. There are no refunds for unused hours. Banked hours are not transferable to other students or other programs such as camps.  All "Day Camps", "Holiday Camps", "Summer Camps", and "Specialty Programs" are an extra fee and not included in the Create/Junior DI program membership. During Camps, students cannot come in to do DI hours (they can only come during the normal DI hours.) Parents are responsible to keep track of their hours. We do not track hours but may audit a student's login record if abuse is suspected.

 

Emergency Medical Treatment

In the event of a medical emergency, the Program staff will first use reasonable efforts to contact the parent(s) and/or guardian(s) before administering or authorizing any treatment. However, the Parent understands that Provider does not have medical personnel available at the Facility.  Parent agrees and hereby grants Provider permission to authorize emergency medical treatment, if necessary, to Child. Parent understands and agrees that the Provider assumes no responsibility for any injury or damage which might arise out of or in connection with such authorized emergency medical treatment. Parent further states that there are no undisclosed health-related reasons or problems which preclude or restricts Child from undertaking the Program, and that Child has adequate health insurance to provide for and pay any medical costs that may be attendant as a result of injury to Child. Parent further acknowledges that it is the Parent’s sole responsibility to notify, inform, and update Provider of any medical conditions of Child, including but not limited to known drug and food allergies, known dietary restrictions, and other medical conditions. Please leave an EpiPen/Auvie-Q with your child at all times, if one is required. We are a nut-free facility. Please do not send any food or drinks that contain nut products. We have a zero-tolerance policy for nuts and take allergies very seriously.


Hours of Business

Hours subject to change. Code Ninjas Hillsborough is open for scheduled member hours on select weeknights and weekend days during the school year. Summer Hours may vary. Create and Junior Members cannot use their hours during camps or special events (only during regular hours). Operating Hours subject to change during the summer season vs. school year. Center may close for inclement weather such as snowstorms, flooding, hurricanes, etc. Federal Holidays and holiday weekends are closed, unless otherwise noted. Center may close in the event of suspected COVID-19 exposure so that it may be sanitized. Center may close in the event of power failure. Closures will be communicated via Facebook, & email, and (if possible) a sign on the door as well. Code Ninjas is closed for the last week of Winter Break and the last week of summer. On some holidays we may offer day camps which are at an extra cost.

Child Pickup/Leave Authorization

Students should be picked up promptly at the end of their checked in time. After 15 minutes, students will be scanned in for another hour. If no hours remain, may be subject to late fees. Parents must come inside to get students -or- at least pull up to the sidewalk in their vehicle and call the desk. No child is permitted to run out into the parking lot alone. No child is permitted to wait outside or at a neighboring business for a ride home. Parents/guardians must inform Code Ninjas of the name of the person who is picking up their child on any day when they themselves are not. The “Authorized Pick-Up Person” must be at least 18 years old and may be asked to provide a photo ID to the staff. This authorization shall remain in force until edited or rescinded in writing by the signers of this authorization. Please specify your authorization in the Emergency Contact form section.


Destruction of Property

If a student is observed willfully destroying or damaging property (not by normal wear and tear), Parent assumes responsibility to repair/replace destroyed/damaged property. Code Ninjas franchisees use camera surveillance which records and can determine if a student did willfully damage/destroy property.


Code of Conduct

Code Ninjas Hillsborough's students are expected to follow our Code of Conduct at all times- whether inside the Dojo or online for a class with Code Ninjas. We treat each other, our Sensei's, our Dojo, and equipment with respect at all times. There is no bullying, hitting, pushing, shoving, name-calling, screaming, cursing, threatening, cyber-bullying, hazing, demeaning, racism, bias, or otherwise mistreating any fellow students or employees. Students must follow the rules of the Dojo and work on the Curriculum, not go on exterior websites/games without staff permission. Absolutely no profanity, explicit images/videos, or texts/messages tolerated. We have a family-friendly atmosphere and positive culture in the Dojo. We want all our students to feel safe, supported, and happy here. Violation of the Code of Conduct can result in: Time Out, Parent Phone Call/Conference, or Student Suspension/ Student Termination without refund (depending on severity).
